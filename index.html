Inside TWS we teach a 4 Layer AI Supercycle Portfolio Framework for a simplified way to build your long term portfolio. A video explaining how to use this effectively is available inside Skool.
① Pick Your Profile
Choose the portfolio that matches your Wealth Risk Profile: Defensive Growth, Aggressive Growth, or High Conviction. Use the tabs above to switch between them.
② Study the Layers
Every portfolio uses the same 4-Layer Framework: Core ETFs → Thematic ETFs → Core Stocks → Asymmetric Bets. Each position shows a primary ticker and a Ticker Option — an alternative expression of the same idea.
③ Make It Yours
The tickers are examples — not guarantees and not orders. The idea is the Layer + Sector. Any stock in the same Layer and Sector inside the Stock Playbook expresses the same idea — swap freely and the framework stays intact.
→ Open the Stock Playbook
④ Time Your Entry
Before entering any position, check it on MidasX.ai: the Value Zone tells you whether the name is in a buy range or extended, and its Dollar Cost Averaging category tells you how to build the position — all at once or staged over time.
